Payroll Outsourcing

MangosOrange is one of the best payroll vendors which offers complete payroll outsourcing services in India. We provide payroll services to small businesses as well as medium and large organisations.

We comply with fully automated processes to handle your payroll outsourcing process so that possibilities of human errors eradicated. We have ensured that our payroll processing follows all the required standard of corporate governance and tax regulations.

We have an excellent Payroll team that has experts working for our clients with years of experience to handle all the payroll needs. Our employees stay with us for the long term as they enjoy their work and are happy with the friendly workplace culture resulting in process continuity. Our subject matter experts are at our clients’ disposal to answer any queries posed to them.
